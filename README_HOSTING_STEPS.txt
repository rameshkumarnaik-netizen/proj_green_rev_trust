GREEN REVOLUTION - NETLIFY HOSTING

1. Keep these files in one folder.
2. Rename your main page as index.html already done.
3. Go to netlify.com and log in.
4. Click Add new site > Deploy manually.
5. Drag and drop this full folder or upload the ZIP.
6. Netlify gives a free URL like https://green-revolution.netlify.app
7. To use your own domain: Site settings > Domain management > Add custom domain.
8. Replace https://your-domain.com in index.html, sitemap.xml, and robots.txt with your real domain.
9. Open Google Search Console and add your domain.
10. Submit https://your-domain.com/sitemap.xml
